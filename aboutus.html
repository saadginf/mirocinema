About us

Welcome to MIRO CINEMA, Upper Hutts’ only boutique Cinema.

A state of the art premium 40 seat Cinema, the MIRO features full digital projection with 7.1 JBL surround sound and premium comfort couches and cushions.

Combined with the latest in Temperzone air conditioning and enhanced sound proofing, your movie experience is something you will keep coming back for.

To compliment that experience, the MIRO is fully licensed with a wide range of beers, wines and spirits. Featuring craft beers such as Panhead and Boneface along with well loved NZ beers and low alcohol options, we have the Mission range of fine wines and your favourite spirits….all at standard bar pricing.

Our fantastic barista made Revive coffee along with hot chocolates, teas and shakes compliment a wide range of Café style food.

Famous MIRO pizzas along with paninis and toasties are a great accompaniment for our slices, cakes and deserts.

Locally owned and operated with great staff and a unique relaxed atmosphere, the MIRO is Upper Hutt's own Cinema experience.
